We Believe in Every Child's Potential

We believe that every child has the right to a safe and happy home in which they can develop to the best of their ability. In Nigeria, many children do not grow up in an environment which enables them to reach their full potential in life. The most vulnerable children, those least likely to have the loving support they deserve, are those with physical and mental challenges, children with HIV/AIDS, and children who have been, for whatever reason, abandoned by their families.

The Ebunoluwa Foundation aims to create an environment in Nigeria in which orphans and vulnerable children get the support they need to have a full, productive, and satisfying life. We do this through three major programme branches:

  • We partner with the Eruobodo House home for orphaned and vulnerable children.
  • We provide scholarships and medical assistance to those who need it and mediate in adoptions to families who can care for these children.
  • We advocate on behalf of these children both at the community and state level for changes to policies and attitudes towards these children.

On this website, you will find information on children in Nigeria, and about our current work, our past achievements, and our future goals. The website also aims to provide a portal to groups and individuals around the world who share our desire to support children who are most vulnerable to neglect.
